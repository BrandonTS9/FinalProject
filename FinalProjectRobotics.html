ROBOTICS

    A robot is a computer that is designed to complete one or more specific tasks. Most of the time, robots have sensors that aid them in the completion of their task. An example of a robot is Spot, the walking dog robot created by Boston Dynamics. Its purpose is vague, but it is able to perform many tasks from military scouting to being a helping hand in an office environment. Its position and force sensors allow it to easily balance itself making crossing rough terrain a possibility. Another example of a robot is a microwave. The microwave's job is to heat up a human's food for a specific amount of time. A microwave might also have sensors that detect a fire or smoke which triggers a mechanism that stops heating the food and sets off an alarm.
